This FREE app combines the power of the most popular music streaming, song recognition and video services into one and only app! This app can automatically recognize any music song then display the title, artist, year, lyrics, and in bonus, the music video that goes with that song!

When running this app, your music experience is enhanced by automatically playing the associated video in sync with the music that is currently playing on your phone or in your envrionment.

With this app you can:
- Automatically recognize what songs are playing around you, on your phone, your radio, or even in the public space you are in
- Let you instantly *view* the music you're listerning to in addition to hearing it
- Turn your phone/tablet into a karaoke machine or video jukebox
- Listen to your favotire playlists or radios while casting the videos on your TV
- Watch the music videos while using your favorite streaming service
- Turn any regular radio station into a music video channel
- Add music video to Spotify or any other streaming service

The app's main features are:
- Listen to your microphone continously to detect any playing song
- Automatically stream the associated music video of the current song
- Continuously and precisely synchronize the video with the music in real-time (results vary for different songs and videos)
- Display the song title, artist and year of release
- Display synchronized lyrics (subtitles)
- Automatically go to a new video when a new song is being detected.
